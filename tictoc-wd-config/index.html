Watchface color options
Choose the background and text colors for the watchface. (For Pebble and Pebble Steel, select from black & white colors only. Black is default for background color and white is default for text color.)
Weather options
Check to enable shake option to show today's lo and hi temperatures. Also displays last weather update time on date line.
Check to display temperature in degrees Celsius. Uncheck to display in degrees Fahrenheit.
Select weather information update frequency via GPS (minutes). Note: Change the weather frequency to force a weather update.
Display options
Check to display current date information.
Check to display current time in digital format.
Check to display dots at the 3, 6, & 9 positions. Relaunch watchface to apply display option.
Check to display weather, date & digital time lines using a larger font size. Relaunch watchface to apply display option.
